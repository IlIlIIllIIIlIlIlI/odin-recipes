Chicken

Description

These baked buttermilk chicken tenders are a healthy alternative to fried chicken. Buttermilk helps to tenderize the chicken resulting in juicy, moist, and tender meat. Serve with your choice of dipping sauce. We particularly enjoy these with honey mustard sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, split into wings, breasts, thighs, and drumsticks
  • 1 cup (240 ml) buttermilk
  • 2 cups (480 ml) breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) smoked paprika
  • Â½ tablespoon (7.5 ml) celery salt
  • 2 teaspoons (10 ml) garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) salt
  • 2 teaspoons (10 ml) pepper
  • 3 tablespoons (45 ml) canola oil

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 475 F (245 C).
  2. Add chicken pieces to a bowl. Pour buttermilk over top. Toss to coat.
  3. To a plastic freezer bag, add breadcrumbs, smoked paprika, celery salt, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix to combine.
  4. Add chicken pieces to bag and shake to coat.
  5. Place breaded chicken onto a greased baking tray.
  6. Drizzle canola oil over top of chicken
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, turning once halfway.
  8. Remove from oven.
